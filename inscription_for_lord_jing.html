This is a transcription of dirge from the Han dynasty(206BCE-220 CE), edited by Avery Clare.

The inscription was established in the Ren County任城 in 143 AD.

The main content of the stele inscription is written in clerical script隸書. The epigraph title is written in seal script篆書.

Each column contains 33 characters.

Height: 230 cm

Width: 78 cm

Columns: 17

Inscription for Han Former Governor of Yi Region and Counselor of Beihai Commandery Lord Jing

In the second year of Han'an Reign (143 C.E.)

autumn □□

Lord Jing, the former governor of Ren County in Beihai Commandery passed away.

Alas, how grievous!

The state □□ treasure

The outstanding scholars lost their peer.

All constellations lost the essence

The latter scholars and the pupils are late in time.

Upon where does Heaven

Arranges the Fate and confers the term.

There is life and there is death

Heaven indeed controls it.

How could that benevolent sage

Be cut down with no remain.

Therefore the former subordinates and students

discussed with each other, said:

As for the great figures of the ancient times

None of which failed to shed light □ infinity,

Spread fragrance and radiance to the records and pieces.

Even if the physical self is dead, the deeds are clear

Even if the body perished, the name continues to exist.

Some have their figures pictured on the graphs,

Some have the odes stroked on the winds and strings.

The latecomers chant their contributions,

Their achievements are recounted on bamboos slips and silks.

Thus composed the dirge, which says:

Prostrating and thinking of the Enlightened Repositor,

Who received his nature from Heaven.

Faithful and deeply virtuous,

He followed the rituals and carried out humaneness.

He pursued the Way and followed the norm,

He cherished the pureness and kept the natural property.

Bright and white, clear and upright

He subdued the self and cultivated himself.

Truly he was gentle and truly he was firm,

A master of sword and pen.

Following father he attended the examination of filial piety,

He received the informal position of Chamberlain for the National Treasury.

Shedding his virtue to Yuan County,

He procured profits and benefitted the people.

Thereupon the brutal subordinates changed their behaviors,

The cornered and feeble ones were covered in benevolence.

He had the prestige established and the beneficence scattered,

He cultivated conducts as if he was a deity.

The emperor admired his deeds,

Summoned him with the tally and decree.

To guard the commanderies of Yi Region,

The journey was far and he was concerned about his parents.

He bent to the marquise and modestly declined

Day and night serving the imperial court.

He proposed loyal and upright advice,

He distinguished the order in the eastern hillside.

The imperial signet superadded the official appointment,

He occupied the position of the Counselor of Beihai Commandery

He governed nineteen cities,

The neighboring countries inclined toward him.

Clearly demarcated the good and the wicked,

He prioritized with respect and concession.

The demolished and the falsified changed mind,

The light and cunning exceeded competition.

The owls did not cry,

The parted children returned to serve the parents.

Multitudes of the widowers and the widows,

They got tranquility because of the blessings.

He accumulated the Way and cultivated his virtue,

□ blessedness to achieve prosperity.

As for the myriad esteemed rituals,

The Enlightened Repositor carried them out.

As for Benevolence and righteousness, the Way and the tactics,

The Enlightened Repositor embraced them.

As for Huang Zhu and Father Shao,

The Enlightened Repositor joined to be the third of them.

As for the official position of the Three Dukes and the buttresses,

The Enlightened Repositor is suitable for them.

In the illness he was summoned,

He entrusted the official post and resigned.

His people □ thought of him ardently,

People from far and near scratched their heads in agitation.

The farmers put down the plow,

The tradesmen left the markets empty,

With every entirety swallowed the tears.

How come the imperial court,

Deprived me of my caring father?

Within ten days after he retired from the office,

His disease became severe and concerning.

The substance of jade-tablet and jade-disc,

Could not be retrieved On the verge of death.

The voices of the woeful whining could not stop,

He passed away suddenly with no delay.

The filial sons crashed and collided,

Toppled and broken as if he was torn apart.

No longer could he be awaken,

He has returned forever to the underworld.

The villagers of the households and people in the prefecture,

Ran to the funeral with tears dripping.

The former subordinates were distressed,

Sobbing and hovering, again and again.

The established scholars with caps and canopies around the state,

Were startled and grieved full of sorrow.

That which has the Great Fate fixed,

Truly it is only given by Heaven.

The enlightened ruler set up the position,

The Enlightened Repositor would not take it.

The vassals and sons desire to serve,

The Enlightened Repositor would not remain.

Alas, how grievous!

The coda says:

Examining the accumulation and the dusky tomb,

To express the extreme □. Oh!

□□□□,

Soared the spirit of Court Gentleman for Consultation. Oh!

Once again appointed the tiger general,

To pacify the common people. Oh!

The cmpass-like stratagems and rule-like advice,

Prioritized allegiance and faith to the master. Oh!

Assisted the guardians and defended the shields,

Appeased the myriad people. Oh!

□□□□,

His grace is more and more brimful. Oh!

He should be promoted to join the tripod vassals,

To fasten the trunk of the pine. Oh!

Not eternal is his lifespan,

He left the vassals and sons behind. Oh!

His benevolence reaches Bohai and Mountain Tai,

People composed Gan Tang for you. Oh!

Engraved on the stone and carved the inscriptions,

□ would not perish. Oh!
